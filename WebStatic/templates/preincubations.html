Pre-incubation

We provide dedicated pre-incubation labs alongside various schools, creating a collaborative space for students to explore and develop innovative ideas.

These labs support early-stage ideation, encourage interdisciplinary learning, and offer the right guidance to help students shape their concepts into viable solutions before moving into full incubation or entrepreneurial ventures.
