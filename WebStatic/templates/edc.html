Entrepreneurship Development Cell

The Entrepreneurship Development Cell (EDC) at VIT enables students to transform ideas into impactful products and ventures. Explore the following schemes, exclusively for current VIT students, to build your Proof of Concept and launch student-led startups.

Product Development Fund (PDF)

Financial support of up to Rs 50,000 to develop a robust Proof of Concept (PoC).

Eligibility: Only applicable for current VIT Students

Student Startup Venture (SSV) Fund

Financial support of up to Rs 5,00,000 to accelerate your student startup venture.

Eligibility: Only applicable for current VIT Students
