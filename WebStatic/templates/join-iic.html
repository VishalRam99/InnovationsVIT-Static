Join Institution's Innovation Council

Be a part of the Institution's Innovation Council (IIC) at VIT and help build a culture of innovation and entrepreneurship on campus. We welcome passionate students across all domains to contribute their skills, creativity, and drive toward meaningful impact.

IIC Recruitments

Recruitments are opening shortly for multiple IIC domains. If you are passionate about innovation, entrepreneurship, and impact, we welcome your application. Submit your application via the Google Form below.

Available Domains

Innovations

Cultivate creativity through hackathons, workshops, and innovation challenges.

Startup

Guide student innovators from idea to execution through mentorship and networking.

IPR

Protect creative ideas and innovations through patent applications.

Events & Operations

Manage smooth execution of events from guest care to crowd management.

Editorial

Tell stories through newsletters, event documentation, and writing initiatives.

Internships

Connect with industry leaders and secure partnerships for VIT students.

Industry Connect

Bridge the gap between students and global industry leaders.

General Guidelines

  • Open to all current VIT students across all years and branches
  • Demonstrate passion for innovation, entrepreneurship, and teamwork
  • Commitment to attend regular meetings and contribute to domain activities
  • Prior experience is not mandatory – we value enthusiasm and willingness to learn
