Student Ideas

Share your innovation with VIT Innovations. This page is open year-round to collect ideas from VIT students. The Institution's Innovation Council (IIC) and our ecosystem evaluate submissions, mentor selected teams, and support them through the journey until developing a working Proof of Concept (PoC).

Who can apply?

Only current students of Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT) are eligible to submit ideas.

How it works

  1. Submit your idea using the form below.
  2. IIC and domain experts review and evaluate submissions on a rolling basis.
  3. Shortlisted teams receive mentorship, access to facilities, and guidance to build a PoC.
  4. Upon successful PoC and validation, projects may be recommended for:
    • Product Development Fund (PDF)
    • Student Startup Venture (SSV) Fund

What to prepare before submitting

  • Problem statement and your proposed solution
  • Innovation/novelty and potential impact
  • Team members, department, and contact details
  • Current stage (idea, prototype, PoC, etc.)
  • Any preliminary validation, references, or supporting links
