Institution's Innovation Council

The Institutions Innovation Council (IIC) is an initiative of the Ministry of Education (MoE), Government of India, launched in collaboration with the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) in 2018. Its aim is to systematically cultivate a culture of innovation and entrepreneurship within India's Higher Education Institutions (HEIs)

IIC Domains

Innovations

Innovations focuses on cultivating a culture of creativity, innovation, and entrepreneurship within the institution. It supports students, faculty, and staff by organizing events like hackathons, workshops, and innovation challenges, while also fostering industry collaborations and providing incubation for startups. The domain encourages research, problem-solving, and the development of new technologies or solutions that address real-world issues. By offering mentoring, networking opportunities, and resources, the Innovations aims to turn innovative ideas into impactful ventures, promoting both academic and entrepreneurial growth.

Startup

The Startup Domain of IIC, VIT Vellore aims to foster an entrepreneurial ecosystem on campus by guiding student innovators from idea to execution. The domain focuses on nurturing early-stage startups ideas through mentorship, workshops, networking events, and collaborations with incubators and industry experts. Turning bold ideas into scalable impact, the Startup Domain stands as the launchpad for the next generation of changemakers and entrepreneurs at VIT.

Intellectual Property Rights (IPR)

The Intellectual Property Rights (IPR) domain focuses on protecting creative ideas and innovations. We work on applying for patents, ensuring that inventors and creators receive proper recognition and legal credit for their work. Our goal is to safeguard originality and promote fair use of intellectual contributions across industries.

Industry Connect

The Industry Connect domain of the Institution's Innovation Council (IIC) at VIT Vellore serves as a strategic bridge between the university and global industry leaders. This domain is dedicated to fostering meaningful engagement with industrialists, C-level executives, influencers, and expert speakers from diverse sectors worldwide. Through a curated calendar of events, panels, and interactive sessions held throughout the academic year, Industry Connect facilitates direct dialogue between these distinguished professionals and VIT’s dynamic student community. The domain plays a pivotal role in enriching the entrepreneurial and professional ecosystem on campus by enabling exposure to real-world insights, mentorship, and collaborative opportunities.
